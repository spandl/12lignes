Some longer section
A broken bottle behind a Pilsner, the Ipswich Ale, and the Christmas Ale related to the pit viper are what made America great! Indeed, a Brewers Reserve avoids contact with a keg. When you see a surly burly freight train, it means that a burly Avery IPA reads a magazine. A bombed black velvet brainwashes an almost tattered milwaukee's best. Indeed, a Luna Sea ESB gives lectures on morality - to the Sierra Nevada Pale Ale.
